About the Course

Generative AI for Work & Research Productivity — Live Online Certification

In this 8‑week program you’ll learn no‑code AI tools for automating data analysis, content creation, knowledge management, and routine tasks. The course combines live workshops, help sessions, and an applied capstone to help you integrate AI into your workflow.

Key Outcomes

  • Automate time‑consuming document, spreadsheet & data tasks
  • Organize literature and knowledge with AI‑powered tools
  • Develop custom chatbots to support projects or teams
  • Create professional visuals & presentations using AI
  • Deliver a real‑world capstone project

Who It's For

Students, faculty, and researchers in health & life sciences who want concrete strategies to be more productive with modern AI tools. No coding experience required.

Logistics
